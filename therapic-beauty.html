Our Mission

Empowering Women Through the Art of Beauty

Therapic Beauty was born from a deep desire to see young women thrive. We believe that every woman — regardless of her background or starting point — deserves the tools, knowledge, and support to build a business she is proud of.

Based in Fourways, Johannesburg, we serve women across South Africa with practical beauty education and premium lash products that give them a real competitive edge in the industry.

  • 🌸 Inclusivity: Our community welcomes women of all backgrounds, races, and experience levels.
  • 💡 Education First: We don't just sell products — we give you the knowledge to use them professionally.
  • 💰 Affordability: Quality training and products should be accessible, not a luxury.
  • 🤝 Community: When you join Therapic Beauty, you join a sisterhood of empowered beauty entrepreneurs.
Meet the founder Nonhlanhla Precious

The Heart Behind Therapic Beauty

Nonhlanhla Precious is a passionate beauty educator and entrepreneur based in Fourways, South Africa. She started Therapic Beauty with one vision: to be the bridge between a young woman's dream and her financial freedom.

Having navigated the beauty industry herself, Nonhlanhla understands the challenges women face when starting out — lack of guidance, expensive products, and no support system. That's exactly what Therapic Beauty solves.

Through hands-on training, honest mentorship, and high-quality products at fair prices, she has helped hundreds of women launch and grow their own beauty businesses.
